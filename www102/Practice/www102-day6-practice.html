Browser Default Styles

Knowing what default styles we inherit from the browser's own stylesheet can make our lives as front-end developers much easier.

Using your chosen browser's developer tools, investigate the default CSS properties and values from the list below, and keep track of what you find in the table.

To aid in your investigation you should use a copy of "The Kitchen Sink" with no custom CSS, Reset or Normalize.

Upload your completed table to your Matrix server space and link as "www102 Day 6 Practice" or a similar name - it's your website after all.
