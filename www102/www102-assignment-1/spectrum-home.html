Welcome to Spectrum Sensory Therapy

We are a multidisciplinary sensory therapy hub that understands the power of embedding care networks in a child's life. Our team of experienced sensory experts, including occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, physiotherapists, psychologists, and dieticians, work together seamlessly to create individualized, integrated programs that address the diverse sensory needs of each child.

At Spectrum Sensory Therapy, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing space where children can discover, explore, learn, play, and thrive, on the journey to better understanding themselves and unlocking their full potential in a supportive environment tailored just for them.

Why sensory therapy?

Sensory therapy can get to the root of various challenges children may be facing, like executive functioning, emotional regulation, picky eating and much more. Our therapists each have varied specialized backgrounds in pediatric care and come together to offer holistic strategies and tools to help better support children through sensory, motor, developmental, emotional and social challenges.

Areas of Focus

  • Daily routine, schedule and habit-building

  • Emotional regulation and stress management

  • Fine and Gross Motor Skills

  • Sensory Processing

  • Social Skills

  • Executive Functioning

  • Developmental Milestones

  • Feeding, Eating Habits and Nutrition

  • Speech, Handwriting and Communication

  • Learning, Accessibility and Assistive Tech Assessments

  • Equipment and environmental modifications

  • Caregiver education and training

Testimonials

The Riviera Family

The Spectrum team has made a significant impact on our lives and an invaluable support to our rainbow baby with cerebral palsy. They set us up with an intricate support system of people who truly care, see and believe in our kid. From sensory integration therapy and aqua therapy, to myofascial release and kinesio taping, to dry needling and muscle stimulation, this clinic has helped our kid gain strength, better posture, balance, flexibility, and most of all, has made them feel more autonomous and confident with every day. We are eternally grateful to have Spectrum Sensory Therapy in our corner.

Principal, Bendale Public School

And we have had more engagement from families and more caregivers wanting to work with us to get to the root of kids' challenges in school and uncover their needs.
