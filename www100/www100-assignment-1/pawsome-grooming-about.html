Who We Are

Pawsome Grooming is a dog grooming salon and spa based in Downtown Toronto. At Pawsome Grooming, we offer a range of services to keep your dogs looking and feeling their best.

Whether you need a simple wash and dry, a full groom, or a special treatment, we have you covered. Our experienced and friendly staff will take care of your dogs with love and care, using only the highest quality products and equipment.

We also have a spacious and comfortable waiting area where you can relax and enjoy some refreshments while your dogs are being groomed.

Our Values

Services

Some of the services we offer include:

  • Bath and blow-dry: We will shampoo and condition your dog's coat, then dry and brush it to remove any loose hair and tangles. This service also includes ear cleaning, nail trimming, and anal gland expression.
  • Full groom: In addition to the bath and blow-dry, we will also trim and style your dog's coat according to your preference or breed standard. We can also do creative grooming, such as coloring, stenciling, or adding accessories.
  • De-shedding treatment: If your dog sheds a lot, this service is for you. We will use a special tool and shampoo to remove the excess undercoat and reduce shedding by up to 90%.
  • Teeth cleaning: We will gently brush your dog's teeth with a toothpaste and toothbrush designed for dogs, then apply a dental spray to freshen their breath and prevent plaque and tartar buildup.
  • Spa package: Treat your dog to a luxurious spa experience, which includes a bath and blow-dry, a full groom, a de-shedding treatment, a teeth cleaning, a facial scrub, a pawdicure, and a massage.

We also offer other services, such as nail clipping, ear plucking, flea and tick treatment, and more. For a full list of services and cost, please visit our website's services and pricing pages.

Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charge.
  • If you cancel or reschedule your appointment within 24 hours of the scheduled time, you will be charged 50% of the service fee.
  • If you do not show up for your appointment or cancel at the last minute, you will be charged the full service fee.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation. Please let us know as soon as possible if you need to change or cancel your appointment. Thank you for choosing Pawsome Grooming! 🐾
